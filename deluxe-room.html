Room Overview

Step into luxury with our Deluxe Room, where 35 square feet of sophisticated elegance awaits you and your companion. From the moment you enter, you'll be greeted by modern furnishings, soft lighting, and panoramic city views that create an ambience of refined comfort. Sink into the sumptuous bedding, unwind in the spacious seating area, or indulge in a refreshing beverage from the well-stocked mini-bar. The spacious en-suite bathroom invites you to relax and rejuvenate with its deluxe bath amenities and rainfall shower. With complimentary Wi-Fi and a host of other premium amenities, the Deluxe Room offers a serene retreat in the heart of the city.
